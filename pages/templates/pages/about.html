About Me

Hi!



“I’m a self taught ambidextrous artist, I’ve always been fascinated by the fact that people can easily express themselves without using words! I vividly remember my first visit to a beach and falling in love with the endless blues! I love how one colour blends into another and makes the vibe of the painting all together different. My work for now is mainly inspired from nature and the people around me!


The calm yet chaotic sound of the rain in the forest, the endless blues of the sea and the desire to escape reality are the things which inspire me. “
